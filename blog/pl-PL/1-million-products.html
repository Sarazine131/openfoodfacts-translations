1 milion produktów i 1 milion podziękowań dla wszystkich uczestników Open Food Facts!

Open Food Facts ma teraz milion produktów spożywczych na całym świecie! Ogromne podziękowania dla dziesiątek tysięcy ludzi, którzy dołączyli do projektu Open Food Facts od 2012 roku, którzy dodali produkty, sfotografowali je i wprowadzili ich informacje do naszej wspaniałej Wikipedii o jedzeniu!

Dzięki Wam udało nam się wspólnie zbudować największą otwartą bazę produktów spożywczych na świecie.

Jest to obywatelska i wspólna baza danych, żywność to sprawa każdego - nie tylko przemysłu rolno-spożywczego czy władz publicznych - i wszyscy możemy uczynić ją bardziej przejrzystą.

It is a free, open and public database, because information on food products is of public interest and it thus must be public and freely reusable by everyone for all purposes: understanding labels and comparing products for consumers, but also scientific research, journalistic investigations, data-driven public policies, etc.

It is a non-profit, collaborative database, independent of the industry, to ensure that the general interest remains our only guide.

It is an open base for all types of product information, in which we are adding more and more fields to more and more domains, so that it can address all the issues related to food such as those which impact on our health, the environment and society.

It is a global database, to address these issues globally, and to encourage the dissemination of best practices around the world.

Open Food Facts is a great example that any one of us can be a changemaker:

  • with her or his own choices using the Open Food Facts mobile app or one of the many other applications that use our database.
  • by participating in the construction of a common good, adding products or completing information on Open Food Facts.

Reaching 1 million products is a great success and a great reward for the efforts of each of us for the last 7 years. We can collectively be very proud of it. A million thanks to all of you who are involved in the project or supporting it!
