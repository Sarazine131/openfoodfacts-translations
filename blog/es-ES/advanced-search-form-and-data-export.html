At the top right of Open Food Facts is now an Advanced Search link. It provides access to a search form where you can specify many criteria to filter products and select only those that contain or do not contain a brand, a label, an origin, a packager code, an additive etc.

You can also set limit values for each element of the nutritional composition: energy, protein, fat, carbohydrates, and all minerals and vitamins.

You will also find at the end of this form a button to download the data in csv format . You can read this format in Excel, LibreOffice Free Software, Open Refine, and other software.

Este es el punto de los datos abiertos: están disponibles en formatos que permiten una fácil reutilización de datos. Puede ordenar, agregar, comparar, etc.

Si ve cosas interesantes en los datos, o si detecta errores, por favor háganoslo saber. :-)

Technical details (which may be requested when opening the data by your software if it does not automatically recognize the format):
- character encoding: UTF-8
- separation of fields by tabs (tab)
